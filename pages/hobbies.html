My Hobbies

Programming

I have always been interested in technology and computers, which is why I decided to pursue a career in programming. I enjoy working on both frontend and backend development, and I am always looking for new projects to work on.

Video Games

Video games have been a big part of my life since I was a kid. I enjoy playing a wide variety of games, from action and adventure to role-playing and strategy games. Recently, I have been playing a lot of indie games and especially enjoy games like Satisfactory and Factorio.

Reading

Reading is a great way to relax and unwind after a long day. I enjoy reading a wide variety of genres, from science fiction and fantasy to historical fiction and non-fiction. I am always looking for new books to add to my collection. My latest read was Rewire Your Brain by John B. Arden.
